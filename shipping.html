Shipping Details

Some important notes

  • Shipping within the Western Cape is free
  • Shipping outside of the Western Cape (but still in South Africa) adds R100 to your check out fee
  • International shipping adds R250 to your checkout fee

Orders take up to a week to complete which we will then notify you of shipment

We also offer express shipment upon order completion (not applicable to local orders)
